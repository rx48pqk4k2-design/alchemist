Our Story

The Living Heritage

Rooted in traditional sachet culture, the collection brings together hand embroidery, natural botanicals, and restrained Eastern aesthetics. Each piece is shaped as a quiet object of protection, fragrance, and everyday ritual.

Handcraft

A Labor of Three Days

Each sachet is completed by hand, from the first stitched outline to the final knot and tassel. Mulberry silk, botanical filling, and fine embroidery are brought together with patience and precision, preserving a craft language shaped by time, material, and human touch.
